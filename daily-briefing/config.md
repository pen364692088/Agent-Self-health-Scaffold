# Daily Briefing Configuration

## Topics & Priority
1. **Economics** - Market movements, major economic indicators, trade policies
2. **Warfare/Conflict** - Major conflicts, geopolitical tensions, defense developments  
3. **Policy** - Government decisions, regulatory changes, international agreements
4. **Technology** - Breakthrough innovations, major tech company news, infrastructure developments
5. **AI** - Model releases, industry applications, safety/ethics developments
6. **Gaming** - Major releases, industry shifts, esports news

## Sources Strategy
- Economics: Financial Times, Bloomberg, Reuters Business
- Warfare: Reuters World, AP International, BBC News
- Policy: Government official sources, major news outlets policy sections
- Technology: TechCrunch, The Verge, Ars Technica
- AI: Industry blogs, research publications, major company announcements
- Gaming: Industry publications, major platform announcements

## Output Format
- Markdown with clear sections
- Top 3 items per category
- Brief summary + key implications
- Source links for verification
- Generation timestamp
