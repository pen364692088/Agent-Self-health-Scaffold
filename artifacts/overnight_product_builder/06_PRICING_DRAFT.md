## Pricing Model Recommendation

### Option A: Usage-Based (Recommended for MVP)

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 5 releases/month, basic outputs |
| Pro | $19/month | 50 releases/month, all outputs, priority support |
| Team | $49/month | Unlimited releases, team collaboration, custom templates |

**Rationale**:
- Low barrier to entry (free tier)
- Scales with usage
- Easy to understand

### Option B: Seat-Based

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Solo | $9/month | Single user, unlimited releases |
| Team | $29/seat/month | Team features, collaboration |
| Enterprise | Custom | SSO, SLA, dedicated support |

**Rationale**:
- Predictable revenue
- Aligns with team-based purchasing

## Willingness to Pay Analysis

Based on research:
- Engineering teams spend 2-4 hours per release on documentation
- Hourly cost of engineer: $50-150
- Value per release: $100-600 saved
- Willingness to pay: $10-50/month

---

## Pricing Strategy

### MVP Phase
- Start with free tier + $19/month Pro
- Collect usage data
- Validate willingness to pay

### Growth Phase
- Introduce Team tier ($49/month)
- Add enterprise features
- Consider annual discounts (20% off)
