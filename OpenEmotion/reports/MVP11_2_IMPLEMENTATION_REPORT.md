## Risk Notes

1. **Mock Planner Limitation**: The current mock planner may not exhibit different behavior under interventions, resulting in near-zero effect sizes. This is expected with the mock planner. Real effect sizes require the full planner integration.

2. **Seed Sensitivity**: With the mock planner, different seeds may produce identical focus sequences. The determinism tests verify internal consistency rather than seed-dependent variation.

3. **CI Time**: The full profile (1000 ticks × 3 runs × 5 conditions) may take 10-30 seconds. The CI profile (200 ticks × 1 run) is faster (~2 seconds).
